Projects

Light City Chronicles

Light City Chronicals is an interactive comic about the theory of light made inside of Unity. It was designed to be experienced on a mobile device.

My contributions to the comic include being the Lead Programmer, Lead Gameplay Designer, concepting, interactivity design, and some panel art.

Green Thumb

Green Thumb is a precision 2D platformer that was create inside of Unity for my first ever Game Jam. The Jam lasted 6 days and was themed around the word "Growth"

My contributions to the game include being the Lead Programmer, Lead Gameplay Designer, and Level Designer

Rat Attack

Rat Attack is a 2D platformer mixed with AR. It was my first big group project and AR project that we developed in around 3 months. The AR was done with the Vuforia Unity plugin using an AR camera and image targets.

My contributions to the game include being lead AR developer, programmer and putting the entire project together.

Dungeon Runners

Dungeon Runners is a 2D platformer that was my first ever game I made inside of Unity. It was created individually during my GIMM 110 class and took me around 3 months to complete.

My contributions to the game include everything such as programming, level design, and art.
